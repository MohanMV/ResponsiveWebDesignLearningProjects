The Legendary John Carmack

The man who revolutionized video games

Here's a timeline of John Carmack's life:

  • 1970 - Born in Shawnee Misson, Kansas.
  • 1980s - Dropped out of university and pursued contract programming jobs. This later led him to accepting a position at Softdisk, a software publishing firm.
  • 1990 - Created the first of many Commander Keen games with fellow colleagues at Softdisk.
  • 1991 - Carmack and his colleagues who worked on the Commander Keen game left Softdisk and started their own company called id Software.
  • 1992 - id Software released Wolfenstein-3D, one of the best first-person shooter games at that time.
  • 1993 - Carmack's company released DOOM, another successful first-person shooter game.
  • 1996 - id Software released their first 3D model game unlike the first 2 games which used 2D sprites that would give an illusion of 3D.
  • 2013 - Carmack joined Oculus VR as their CTO and a few months later resigned from id Software.
  • 2019 - Carmack stepped down from his role as CTO to become a Consulting CTO in order to focus more of his time in Artifical General Intelligence(AGI).

"Focused, hard work is the real key to success. Keep your eyes on the goal, and just keep taking the next step towards completing it. If you aren't sure which way to do something, do it both ways and see which works better." - John Carmack
